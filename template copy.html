Greetings from Team CultRang!

As a part of the IIT Goa family, the CultRang organizing team is delighted to inform you that the third edition of our cultural fest, CultRang'22, will be taking place from 21th to 23th April, and we are eagerly looking forward to your enthusiastic participation in the same.

We aim to make CultRang an illustration of talent that brings together different organisations and students, and ties them with a bond that goes deeper than a mere professional acquaintance and beyond conventional boundaries. We vow to give you an euphoric wonder and delighted articulation of life and fulfill the touchstones of our humble piece of heaven on earth, where the ocean meets the land.

We have a wide range of competitions and workshops lined up to make this a memorable experience for you!

This time attributable to the Cultural Fest, you can come along with us from the solace of your homes. And what's more? All this is completely free of cost!

Yes! There is no registration fee, but there are Exciting Prizes to be won by those who emerge victoriously!
